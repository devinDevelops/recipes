Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Get ready to indulge in the best homemade chocolate chip cookies you can imagine!

Ingredients

  • flour
  • butter
  • eggs
  • baking soda
  • granulated sugar
  • brown sugar
  • chocolate chips
  • vanilla

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Mix flour and baking soda
  3. Mix butter and sugars in a seperate container from flour. Add vanilla and eggs.
  4. Gradually mix in flour to butter and sugar mix
  5. Divide dough into desired size
  6. Place on baking sheet with parchment paper and put in the oven
  7. Bake for 12min or until golden brown.
  8. Take out and let cool
  9. Enjoy!
