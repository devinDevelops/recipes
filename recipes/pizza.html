Pizza

Description

We'll be creating a homemade pizza that will fill your kitchen with the aroma of an Italian pizzaria!

Ingredients

  • pizza dough
  • marinara sauce
  • red onion
  • mozzarella
  • fresh basil
  • extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F
  2. Roll the dough out into your desired shape and thinness
  3. Spread a thin layer of EEVO on the dough
  4. Spread your desired amount of marinara sauce
  5. Shred the mozzarella and place on top. Break chunks off and place those
  6. Slice the red onion and place desired amount
  7. Lightly drizzle EEVO over top
  8. Place pizza on tray/pizza stone and put it in the oven
  9. After 18min, or until crust is golden brown, take the pizza out
  10. Top pizza with fresh basil, whole or chopped
  11. Enjoy!
