Chicken Tacos

Description

A quick and easy recipe for a taste of Mexican cuisine!

Ingredients

  • chicken breast/thighs
  • tortillas
  • tomato
  • red onion
  • avocado
  • cilantro
  • limes
  • olive oil
  • spices
    • cumin
    • garlic powder
    • paprika

Steps

  1. Drizzle olive oil on chicken and add spices. Squeeze half a lime in and mix well
  2. Drizzle olive oil in a pan and bring to medium-high heat
  3. Once chicken reaches 165°F, remove from heat and let cool
  4. Place tortialls in pan. Heat until warm and remove
  5. Dice up the chicken into bite-sized pieces and place in tortialls
  6. Dice the tomato, red onion, and cilantro. Top the chicken tacos
  7. Dice avocado and add to the chicken tacos
  8. Garnish with extra cilantro and lime juice
  9. Enjoy!
